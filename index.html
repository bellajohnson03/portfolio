About Me

I am a Homeland Security student specializing in Emergency Management and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). My work focuses on applying geospatial analysis to understand hazards, critical infrastructure, and community resilience. Through academic projects and GIS experience, I have developed skills in ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, StoryMaps, spatial analysis, geospatial data management, and cartographic design. My projects emphasize the use of open-source data to support emergency planning, risk assessment, and data-driven decision-making.
